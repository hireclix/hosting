What is Jobvite Refer?

Jobvite Refer provides Schneider Electric employees with a fast and easy-to-use tool for sharing job openings within their social networks. Help our recruiters find the perfect candidate, promote the Schneider Electric brand, and help us make our next big hire.

We know that learning a new product can sometimes be a challenge, so we've included three easy steps to help you hit the ground running.

Enable Publisher

Publisher allows every employee to share company job openings with their own social networks. Learn how to find, enable, and set Jobvite Publisher to share your postings with your personal networks.
