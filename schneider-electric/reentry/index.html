Re-entry Program Name

Schneider Electric is looking for experienced professionals who took an employment break of three or more years in their field and are looking to re-launch their careers. The NAME program is a 6 month professional paid internship through which you’ll polish your skills, acquire new ones, and restore the confidence you need to launch a new phase in your professional journey.

Take advantage of comprehensive training, technical and professional development opportunities and networking. NAME will prepare you for a full-time career in critical roles at Schneider Electric, the global specialist in energy management and automation.

Schneider Electric Partners with SWE & iRelaunch

The Society of Women Engineers, in partnership with re-entry firm iRelaunch, are promoting employment opportunities within the STEM Re-entry Task Force initiative to engineers who are interested in getting back to their technical careers. Schneider Electric is one of the six companies partnering with SWE and iRelaunch to start their program in 2017.
Visit http://reentry.swe.org/internship-opportunities for more information.
