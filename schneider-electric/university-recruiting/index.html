Sales Development Program (SDP)

  • 12-month program, two locations

  • Majors: Engineering, Construction Management, Business w/ Project Management or Sales Specialization

Customer Care Center Technical Consultant

  • 12-18-month program, one location

  • Majors:Computer Science, Information Technology, Computer Networking, any Engineering, and other technical degrees

Internship Program

  • 10 weeks in the Summer, up to one full year, part- or full-time

  • Variety of majors, including most Business and Engineering specialties

Finance Advanced Development Program (FADP)

  • 3-year rotational program, three years, three locations

  • Majors: Finance, Accounting, Economics

Global Supply Chain Advanced Development Program (GSC-ADP)

  • 2-yr rotational program, four positions, two locations

  • Majors: Industrial, Manufacturing, Mechanical, or Electric Engineering, Supply Chain; Manufacturing or Supply Chain Internship preferred

Customer Care Center Sales Consultant

  • 5-12-month program, one location

  • Majors: Business Management or Administration, Selling & Sales Management, Selling & Marketing, Marketing, all Business Majors (+ Liberal Arts) with Sales interest

Why Schneider Electric?

We're a dynamic, global company looking for passionate people to help us innovate at every level. We are champions of safe, reliable, efficient, sustainable and connected energy for everyone. From simple switches to complex operational systems, our solutions augment the way our customers manage day-to-day operations. Our connected technologies reshape industries, transform cities and enrich lives.
