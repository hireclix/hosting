Why USA TODAY NETWORK?

USA TODAY NETWORK stands as one of the top 20 digital companies in the country with a loyal following of readers who value fact-based journalism, commitment to serve local communities, and innovative content delivery.

 

Beyond one Team

People say it takes a village, but USA TODAY accomplishes its goals by leveraging a network. Within each team, there’s a mix of creatives and technical whizzes who come together to brainstorm, investigate, produce, and publish stories that inform and engage. Even though there are a ton of moving parts, employees are given space to tackle projects and add unique qualities to deliverables.

 

LEDE-ING THE WAY

The management style at USA TODAY NETWORK differs from team to team, but flexibility and an easy-going nature are consistent across the board. Though the newsroom is often buzzing with stories and excitement, leaders set great examples of how to treat employees and hit deadlines—all while keeping tabs on trends and sharing big-picture goals with everyone on board.

 

PUTTING PEOPLE FIRST

The newsroom comes with plenty of excitement, so employees choose casual gatherings over adventurous off-sites. Taking walks around the campus pond or heading to local eateries are standard—but no one turns down Wednesday Fun Days, especially if breakfast or special events are involved. Employee Awards are also a favorite, and the GoForward Committee that organizes employee resource groups offers a slew of additional perks.
