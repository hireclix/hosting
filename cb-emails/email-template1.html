On October 1, we will launch a new bank-wide employee referral program –
One Commerce Team Referral program.

External research and our own internal data show that referral hires have longer tenure and are better performers than hires from other external sources. Thus, we are focusing on increasing our hires from referrals by launching and actively promoting the new One Commerce Team Referral program.
The program has three simple steps:
Refer – Encourage your qualified friends, family, and former colleagues to apply for positions on the Commerce career site and enter your name as the referring employee.
Reward – Receive awards when your referral is invited to an interview and hired.
Relax – Encourage more qualified individuals to apply for jobs at Commerce.
This new program rewards employees for referral hires with the choice of a cash bonus or additional paid time off on a tiered scale by the level of the new hire:

Individual employees can select either the cash or additional PTO as their reward, with PTO be subject to management approval. You can obtain more details about the program, rewards, policy, or process, visit the One Commerce Team Referral program intranet page or download this executive presentation.

We will be communicating this new program to all employees starting on September 17, 2018. Please help us encourage employees to participate by sharing the program details with your direct reports, sending messages to employees, or even referring a candidate yourself. You can find presentations and sample messages in the Manager’s Toolkit.
Thank you for your assistance as we launch this new program.
