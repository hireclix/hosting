Pet Partners’ hospitals range in size from one to two doctor general practices, 24 hour emergency clinics and large referral centers. Regardless of the size, hospitals in the Pet Partners’ community retain their unique brand. In 2016, Pet Partners joined the Mars, Inc. family of businesses, and today it is more than 85 clinics strong throughout the United States.

BluePearl Veterinary Partners is a national provider of specialty and emergency veterinary care. The national network of hospitals offers advanced specialty services, with most being open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week to treat the most critical emergencies. In 2015, BluePearl joined the Mars, Inc. family of businesses, and today it is more than 60 hospitals strong.

Founded as a small companion animal practice in Portland, OR, Banfield has been focused on preventive care since its inception in 1955 and has grown to become the largest veterinary practice in the world. In 2007, Banfield joined the Mars, Inc. family of businesses, and today it is more than 1000 hospitals strong in neighborhoods across the United States and Puerto Rico.

Founded by a veterinarian, Royal Canin is a leader in science-based cat and dog health nutrition with 50 years of experience in delivering individualized nutritional solutions. Since July 2002, Royal Canin has been part of Mars, Inc., and currently has products available in over 90 countries. The Royal Canin product line offers a wide range of diets based on size, age, breed, lifestyle and therapeutic requirements for the specific needs of the cats and dogs.
