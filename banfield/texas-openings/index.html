A Few Fun Facts

We have several hospitals located in Austin and San Antonio, two of the most exciting cities in Texas! With so much to do and see outside of our hospitals walls, by working at Banfield you can easily balance work and play.

Did you know…

  • According to Forbes Austin is the fastest growing city in the United States
  • In 2017, Austin was named best place to live in the United States by the U.S. News and World Report
  • Austin is one of the best cities for dogs! With countless dog friendly restaurants, trails, parks, and beaches, you can soak up every moment with your pup.
  • Forbes Traveler rated Austin among the top bicycling cities in America
  • Austin is the place for music lovers, and is known as the Live Musical Capital of the World™
  • From October to June you can catch a San Antonio Spurs Game at the AT&T Center
  • San Antonio is home of the 700 ft Tower of the Americas, build for the World’s Fair in 1968 which overlooks the city
  • The San Antonio Zoo is part of an elite group of humane certified ™ zoos
  • In 2016, the U.S. Census Bureau named San Antonio the 7th fastest growing city

Things to do Austin

  • Float in the Grotto Hamilton Pool, a natural swimming pool created from a collapsed underground river
  • Take a dip in the Barton Springs Pool, a 70 degree, spring-fed swimming hole in Zilker Park
  • Stroll down Congress Bridge and experience the 1.5 million Mexican free-tailed bats that emerge at dusk
  • Hike, bike, and boat along Lady Bird
  • Learn the real story of Texas at the famous Bullock Texas State History Museum
  • Explore 1.5 miles of incredible natural underground weathering rock at Longhorn Cavern State Park
  • Catch a show at the historic Paramount Theater

Get active in San Antonio

  • Meander past the countless restaurants and shops located on the San Antonio Riverwalk
  • Step back in time at the San Antonio Missions Park, home of the Alamo
  • Take in 5,000 years of history at the San Antonio Museum of Art
  • Visit The Main Plaza, and experience community farmers markets, concerts, and more
  • Take in 38 acres, 250 plant species, and countless trails at the San Antonio Botanical garden
  • Discover a Safari right at home at the National Bridge Wildlife Ranch where you can visit zebras, giraffes and more
