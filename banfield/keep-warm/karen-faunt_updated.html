Dear Dr. [recipient-last-name],
 
For as long as I can remember, I wanted to be a veterinarian. Like me, you have chosen to dedicate your life – and career – to helping pets. It’s because of this choice, that I’m personally driven to continuously help develop and improve veterinary quality standards, not only for my pets, but for our hospital associates who serve the pets and clients we see everyday.
 
I believe quality touches everything; it shapes the care and the client experience we provide and of course, our engagement with the work we do each day. From the moment a client walks through our doors, every step we take — from taking accurate weights to understanding their pet’s history and needs — ensures we are delivering quality care to each pet.
 
Having standards and protocols in place isn’t simply an example of our commitment to quality but allows us to provide the best possible and individualized care for each pet seen. I encourage your to take a look at our Anesthesia Medical Quality Standards for a deeper dive on the quality of our anesthesia practices. Built on a solid foundation of evidence-based standards and protocols, our anesthesia medical quality standards, provide sound operational practices, a focus on patient safety, and are enforced through robust team member training and development programs.
 
At Banfield, we work hard to give back to the profession by sharing and promoting the knowledge gained through millions of pet visits in our hospitals each year. We strive to build better systems and processes, through the right formulary equipment and support so, you can bring your best self to work every day while providing the individualized care each pet deserves.
 
Thank you for choosing Banfield! I am thrilled to welcome your knowledge, passion, and unique perspective to our practice! I know you will help us continue to make progress on our quality journey, driving veterinary medicine forward, and enabling us to provide great care to even more pets.
 
 
Karen Faunt, DVM, MS, Dipl. ACVIM small animal
