Dear Dr. [recipient-last-name],
 
I imagine, like me, you’ve wanted to be a veterinarian for as long as you can remember. We have dedicated our lives – and now careers – to helping pets. It’s why I’m driven to continuously help develop and improve our quality standards, not only for my pets, but for our hospital associates who serve our pets and clients every day.
 
I believe quality touches everything; it shapes the care we provide, our clients’ experience and our engagement. The moment a client walks through our doors with their pet, every step — from taking accurate weights to understanding a pet’s history and the client’s needs — ensures each pet receives quality care.
 
At Banfield, we work hard to give back to the profession by sharing and promoting knowledge gained through the millions of pet visits in our hospitals each year. We strive to build better systems and processes, as well as have the right formulary, equipment and support that let you be your best every day as you provide the individualized care each pet needs and deserves.
 
This summer, we are releasing the next edition of our anesthesia book, which outlines the fundamentals of quality and clinical essentials around anesthesia and pain management. Having standards and protocols, while encouraging individualization of care, helps lead to the best outcomes for each pet we see and is just one example of our commitment to quality. Also, take a look at the results of our Anesthesia Study in the March 15 Journal of the American Veterinary Medical Association!
 
Thank you for choosing Banfield. I am thrilled to welcome you and your knowledge, passion and unique ways of looking at veterinary care to the practice. I know you will help us continue to make progress on our quality journey, enabling us to provide great care to even more pets.
 
 
Karen Faunt, DVM, MS, Dipl. ACVIM small animal
