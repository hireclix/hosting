We know that a passion for pet wellbeing is why you chose this profession. At Banfield, we believe it’s just as important to support the wellbeing of those who devote their lives to this cause. As such, we are developing a holistic wellbeing strategy that includes five essential elements: Physical, Mental, Community/Social, Career and Financial.
 
 
As part of our financial well-being, we believe we have a responsibility to help veterinarians address the burden of student debt. High levels of student debt are plaguing the industry and in response, we are thrilled to share that Banfield is launching three new revolutionary student debt relief programs this December that will meaningfully support our doctors.

These programs are designed to create an environment where our doctors can focus on what matters to them the most - helping pets and practicing high-quality medicine. Please see below for high-level details on the new offerings.
 
Refinancing Option
  • A low interest loan refinancing option with an additional .25% point interest rate reduction 
  • This benefit will be available to new, returning, and existing full-time doctors who qualify for refinancing
Student Programs Reward
  • A payment of $2,500 for each Banfield student program the associate participated in prior to graduation up to a total of $10,000
  • This benefit will be available to doctors joining Banfield within 18 months of graduation on or after December 2017
  • Payments will be made directly to qualifying student loans after 12 months of employment
Monthly Student Loan Contribution
  • A payment of $150/month paid directly to qualifying student loans
  • This benefit will be available to new and existing FT doctors holding student debt
We are dedicated to continually improving ourselves and the veterinary profession. We’re excited to invite you to take another look at Banfield. We think you’ll like the changes you see! If you are interested in learning more about these programs or opportunities, check us out on Banfield.com/careers.
 
Thank you for the work you do every day to make a better world for pets!
