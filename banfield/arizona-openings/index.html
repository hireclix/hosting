Did you know…

  • Arizona is home to many universities including University of Arizona, Arizona State University, Northern Arizona University, and Grand Canyon University
  • The Grand Canyon is located just a few hours north of Phoenix, a great place for hiking, camping, and sightseeing
  • You can get your year round sports fix with the Arizona Diamondbacks, Phoenix Suns, Phoenix Mercury, Phoenix Rising, Arizona Cardinals, and Arizona Coyote
  • Arizona is an ideal state for dogs, with animal friendly hotels, restaurants, hikes, and campsites
  • The weather can’t be beat in Arizona with over 300 days of sunshine and 65 days of only slightly less sunshine

Things to do...

  • Explore 286 acres of radiant red rock at Monument Valley which is just five miles west of Sedona
  • Visit Arizona-Sonora Desert Museum, one of the “Top 10 Museums” in the U.S. according to TripAdvisor.com
  • Spring Training baseball is HOT in Arizona and you can check out 10 MLB teams as they prepare for their professional season
  • Take a stroll down the London Bridge, yes the real London Bridge which was dismantled from the River Thames and relocated to Arizona in 1967

Get active…

  • Hike Camelback Mountain, Humphrey’s Peak, Mount Lemon, and so many more beautiful mountains
  • Skii or snowboard the Arizona Snowbowl in Flagstaff
  • Hop in an innertube and float for miles down the Salt River
  • Step back in time at the San Xavier del Bac Mission in Tucson
  • Take in 24 million acres/feet of water by boat, WaveRunner™, Jet Ski™, or kayak at Lake Powell

If you’re looking for sunshine, wildlife, fantastic weather,
and best of all, affordable housing, search no further than Arizona!
