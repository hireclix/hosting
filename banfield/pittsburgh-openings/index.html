Welcome to Pittsburgh!

Big city living with a small town feel

With a centrally located city center, Pittsburgh offers everything from city living, suburbia and rural living within a 20 minute drive in any direction!

Join our Pittsburgh Team!
Nearly all of our Pittsburgh hospitals are within a half hour of each other. This allows our hospital teams to stay connected, feel supported, and easily cover shifts for the expected and unexpected. Through fun local events and networking they collaborate and share best practices each and every day.

Get involved...

From toy and food drives to numerous volunteer events throughout the year, our Pittsburgh teams are always willing to pitch in and help their local communities. Something to note two of our doctors are heavily involved in charitable organizations, Dr. Megan Garrison is president of the PVMA and Dr. Emily Morse is on the board for the Parkway Center Vet Tech Assistant school.

Things to do...

  • Stroll through the fourteen room Phipps Conservatory and the beautiful Botanical Gardens to experience various species of exotic plants
  • Indulge on Restaurant Row which features fine dining, gourmet ice cream, and baked goods
  • Discover four floors worth of interactive exhibits at the Carnegie Science Center
  • You can get your year round sports fix with the Steelers, Penguins, and Pittsburgh Pirates
  • From dog friendly hotels, parks, restaurants, and even baseball games, you’ll never have to leave your fury friend at home
  • The three rivers are only a few of the many waterways attracting kayaks and water sport enthusiasts
