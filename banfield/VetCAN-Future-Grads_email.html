Support from day one.
Our goal is to set you up for success so you have all the information, resources, and tools needed to excel in your career.
 
Your wellbeing matters.
We’re committed to building a culture of health and wellbeing through our multifaceted, robust health and wellbeing strategy. We’ll help you focus on our five essential elements: mind, body, financial, career, and community.
 
Will you have student debt after graduation?
All full-time veterinarians are eligible to enroll in our Veterinary Student Debt Relief Pilot Program which offers:
  • An automatic monthly student loan contribution of $150 paid by Banfield directly to your qualifying student loans
  • A $2,500 payment for each qualifying Banfield student program that you have participated in prior to graduating, up to a maximum of $10,000
  • The option to refinance your loans with a supplementary interest rate reduction of .25% from a third-party institution*
 
The steps you take today towards your career in veterinary medicine could be life changing. If you’re interested in learning more about our Student Job Program, Student Ambassador Program, or externships opportunities, please don’t hesitate to contact me directly.
