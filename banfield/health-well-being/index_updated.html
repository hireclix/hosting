Veterinary Student Debt Relief Pilot

The pilot offers veterinarians: a low-interest loan refinancing option, a $150 monthly practice-paid contribution paid directly to qualifying student loans, and a $2,500 payment for each qualifying Banfield student program which the doctor participates prior to joining the practice.

Additional holiday closures

We will be closing all hospitals on New Year’s Day, providing an additional day away from work for all our associates. Your wellbeing matters and we are constantly evaluating areas where, as a practice, we can improve flexibility and associate engagement.

Wellbeing continuing education

We’re launching virtual trainings of stress resilience and confront conflict with teams practice-wide to help our veterinary teams better understand how job-related stress impacts their physical and mental wellbeing, as well as provide comping mechanisms and tolls to build resilience.
