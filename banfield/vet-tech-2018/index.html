Veterinary Student Debt Relief Pilot

As a full time veterinarian, the pilot offers you: a low-interest loan refinancing option, a $150 monthly practice-paid contribution paid directly to qualifying student loans, and a $2,500 payment for each qualifying Banfield student program in which you participate in prior to joining the practice.

Associate Assistance Program

Our Associate Assistance Program is a resource that supports a wide range of personal issues. Services include unlimited phone, online, and mobile access to experienced professional consultants to talk about concerns (included are five face-to-face sessions with licensed counselors per year). In addition, their award-winning website provides articles, videos, workshops and more.

Wellbeing continuing education

We’re launching virtual trainings of stress resilience and confront conflict with teams practice-wide to help our veterinary teams better understand how job-related stress impacts their physical and mental wellbeing, as well as provide comping mechanisms and tolls to build resilience.
